Kids Meals in our Restaurant

Children can choose from our "Oliver's for Kids" menu (under 12s only).

Priced from just £6.70, all meals include chips plus garden peas, mushy peas or Heinz baked beans, as well as a drink and an ice-cream or lolly. In addition, macaroni cheese and spaghetti bolognese alternatives are available.

You can see the Kids restaurant menu on the main restaurant menu.

For the tiny ones there are high-chairs and baby-changing facilities.
